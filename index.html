The word game for those who think outside the planet.

How to play: We give a word an entirely new definition. Your task is to figure out the word! You are able to ask for 1 letter at a time and you have 6 incorrect guesses to find the whackily redefined word. Select your letter from the keyboard on screen and then press the enter button if you are happy.
When you think you've got it, press the final attempt button and have a crack, but you'll only get one shot at this!
Here is an example:

QUESTION: "Educated, Tense Person"
ANSWER: Taut

Now you know how it works, good luck and there will be a new word waiting for you each day!
